About me

Recent graduate from UCSB with a degree in Chemistry. In the Chemistry labs, I was disappointed with the outdated technology that analyzed the chemicals and my appreciation for technology grew. Taking an intro Python class my last quarter at UCSB, I found myself interested in coding and been at it since.

Since then, I've been learning code with help of friends from UCSB/UCI, from coding meetups such as NSCoder Night, OC iOS group, and FreeCodeCamp. I participated in 3 hackathons and won a hackathon. I was a web developer intern at startup goVyrl. There I've built a custom backend admin portal in JavaScript. I completed Stanford's 193p iOS course and built a portfolio of apps from there. I'm a registered member of Apple's Developer Program. Upwork is an online tech job posting platform and I've scored a 4.5/5 on their Swift test.
